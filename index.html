My Story

Hi, my name is Elsie Howarth and I am a Northumbria University Graduate in Web Design and Development. I am originally from Blackpool and moved to Newcastle to study at university.

Whilst at university I study many modules which specialised in web technologies. I am know confident in languages such as HTML, CSS, PHP, SQL, Javascript and JQuery. I worked hard in my studies and manage to secure myself a first class degree.

I went to university for four years as I completed a placement year as a software developer at HMRC. Once I had finished university, I took a web developer role at a local bathroom company. I then moved on to an energy company which I was also a web developer.

I have had an great experience in Newcastle Upon Tyne so far, making friends, getting my degree and also gaining valuable experience in frontend development roles. The only down side is leaving my family and dogs (the cuties at the top of my page).
